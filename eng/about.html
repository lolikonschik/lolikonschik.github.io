State Research and Design and Research Institute "Research Institute for Reconstruction" was founded in 1945, has 25 territorially separated units (23 branches, 2 branches) in regional centers and large cities of Ukraine, which employ more than 600 specialists.

Institute "Research Institute for Reconstruction" is:

  • - the basic organization for scientific and technical activity in construction (order of the Minregion of Ukraine from 03.05.2012 №193);
  • - the main coordinator and executor of the complex of research and design works on the reconstruction of first-series residential buildings (decision of the State Construction Committee of Ukraine dated 5.11.98 362);

MAIN ACTIVITIES

Research activities:

  • - Housing Policy Development;
  • - reforming housing and communal services;
  • - updating the normative base regarding the reconstruction, repair and operation of housing and communal facilities;
  • - scientific and methodological support of technical developments and projects;
  • - Experimental Design.

Project activity:

  • - comprehensive design of construction, reconstruction and repair;
  • - diagnostics of the technical condition of buildings and structures;
  • - engineering research;
  • - gas and telephony;
  • - redevelopment of premises;
  • - certification of objects;
  • - cottage projects;
  • - monitoring the construction and reconstruction of facilities;
  • - energy audit and energy saving;
  • - Real Estate Expert Appraisal;
  • - approval of project documentation;
  • - information support;
  • - dispatching, modernization of the elevator farm;
  • - consulting services.

According to the projects of the Institute, a significant number of residential buildings and complexes, objects of education, medical and health facilities were reconstructed, including the Diplomatic Academy of the Ministry of Foreign Sleep of Ukraine, the National Philharmonic, the Hrushevsky History and Memorial Museum, the Drama Theater, young spectator (Kyiv); cancer department of the regional children's radiation center (Vinnytsia); Bryansk Cathedral, Post Office (Dnipropetrovsk); The Shipbuilding and Fleet Museum, the Vereshchagin Museum (Mykolaiv), the Old Exchange (Odessa); military housing objects (mm. Bila Tserkva, Rivne, Kherson); Hunting Castle (Cherkasy); Red Square (Chernihiv); radiological corps of the oncology dispensary, the summer theater (Chernivtsi), etc .; experimental construction of a 29-storey complex as part of a hotel-residential-office center, an apartment building, a shopping block and a built-in 4-storey underground parking, 72 Velyka Vasylkivska St., Kyiv; the health complex in the tract Laspi, Crimea; hotel complex on Terletskogo str., Foros village; construction of multi-storey residential buildings with underground parking lots (mm Kyiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Chernihiv, etc.).

Much of the development of the Institute has an investment appeal.

Many years of practice of scientific activity and design of objects of various purpose, high professional level of specialists ensure the performance of the institute and branches of the whole complex of scientific, research and design-research works on construction, reconstruction and repair of objects of different purpose.
